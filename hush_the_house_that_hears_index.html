THE HOUSE THAT HEARS

A sound-horror survival game made from procedural art and generated audio. You are trapped in a house with five sealed mouths in the walls. The flashlight helps you see, but the house hears the click. Running saves seconds, but teaches the thing your rhythm.

WASD move · Shift run · F flashlight · E hold/listen. On mobile, use the stick and buttons.

Single-file. No external assets. Best with headphones.
